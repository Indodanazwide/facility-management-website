Our Story

Facility Management Solutions was founded in 2024 with the goal of improving facility management through technology. Over the years, we have achieved several milestones, including successfully developed and launched the prototype version of our Facility Management Application, demonstrating its core functionalities, incorporated valuable feedback from users and stakeholders to enhance the user experience and functionality of our application, and expanded our services to new regions and markets, catering to a wider range of clients and industries.
